About Us

    Welcome to PointBlanc, our one of a kind Inter-college eSports and Gaming Expo. This is an idea in the making for the past 16 months. We first came up with the idea of promoting eSports on a grand inter college scale for our Year-I presentation project. Ever since then, our minds have been fixated to bring this to reality and here comes the gap we were looking for.

    This Aether 2019, we want to make this dream of ours come true. College level eSports shouldn’t be confined to small classrooms and segregated out of the liveliness of the fest. It should be inculcated into the fest and be made grand; which is why PointBlanc will happen in the open, for both passerby’s and hardcore fans to experience amidst the fun filled fest. Complete with gaming lounge facilities, VR experiences and old school games to try out on the side we want to make sure everyone is welcome into the world of gaming. In addition, we also support up and coming game developers to showcase their work at our event. If you want to demo your game to the world, get in contact with us.

    In short, our goal is simple. Make gaming a big deal, make eSports become a reality in India by going inter college, enable game developers and create an ecosystem of playing and making games for everyone. This is what PointBlanc is about, this is what we are bent on seeing come true.

    Let’s party up! <(￣︶￣)>
